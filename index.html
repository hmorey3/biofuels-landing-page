Algae Data Manager

An sleek portal to assist in the research of algea biofuel processing and allow customers around the globe to track and analyze results.

Learn More

Problem Statement

• Secure, efficient, and intuitive solution to
tracking algae samples.
• Analytical analysis of algae processing and results.
• Customer portal allowing clients to view sample results instantly and make informed decisions.

Intended Use & Audience

In the Biofuels application, Dr. Allen and his research team can easily enter the sample processing data which will be stored in a backend database. After inputting the data, Dr. Allen’s customers will be able to view their results instantly in a central location.

Client Organization

The Biofuels web application was designed for Dr. Todd Allen, a teacher in the Chemistry Department at Liberty University. The application will assist in his research, allowing him to track and analyze algae samples for his customers around the globe.

Features Overview

Homepage

Users can view a live quantity comparison of the Reaction and GC Vials within the batches stored on the application.

Samples

Most algae samples go through here, where users may input the sample data of the batches they receive. Data can be inserted at every step of the process between having a Reaction vial and a GC vial.

Vials

Change the finer details of individual vials here, view preparation details and values, and trace them back to specific samples.

Customers

For a batch to exist on the application, a customer must exist to suffice its creation. This is where users can list them.

Users

Administrators can manage users permitted onto the site and modify the privileges of their account or disable accounts entirely.

Other

Config Here, forms containing important values for future reference can be created and modified.
Invoices Transmit invoices containing sample and vial data from this page; invoices can also be downloaded locally in chart format.
Equip Management Register any lab equipment used so that it may be identified with a vendor.
